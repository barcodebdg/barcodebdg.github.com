    What is Barcode Bandung?

    The blog and community that share tutorial about programming written by our lovely contributors in Bahasa Indonesia. We was founded in Bandung, one of the creative city in Indonesia. Although we put the Bandung city name into Barcode, it doesn't mean we are exclusive to Bandung's programmer. No...no...we are open for everyone to share their knowledge and learn from our blog and community :D

    There are some of our existing contributors, who also lived outside Bandung.

    Do you have some awesome code to share? Tell us! We can make a repo for you or we can fork your code.

    How To Contact Us?

    Just mention @barcodeBdg on Twitter. You can also mention to @bepituLaz or @alfaridi, both of them are the main administrator who maintain and organize the blog.
